# Prisma — Professional Quote Generator

**Prisma** is a free online tool designed to help professionals create **clean, structured commercial quotes** quickly and without unnecessary complexity.

It allows you to build complete proposals with **items, prices, payment conditions, and customer information**, all organized in a professional format ready to send or print.

<Callout>
Live demo: https://useprisma.vercel.app/
</Callout>

## Overview

Prisma was built to simplify the process of generating **commercial proposals and professional quotes**, reducing manual work and minimizing input errors.

It is particularly useful for **freelancers, service providers, and small businesses** that need to generate clear, organized, and consistent quotes for clients.

---

## Key Features

### Quote Structure

Prisma allows you to configure a complete proposal including:

- Quote number
- Proposal validity
- Itemized list with description, quantity, and price
- **Separate shipping field**, clearly distinguishing delivery or travel costs
- Automatic subtotal, discounts, and final price calculations
- Payment method
- Additional notes or conditions

All totals update **automatically in real time** as items are edited.

---

### Sender Information

You can configure your professional details, including:

- Name or company name
- CPF or CNPJ
- Full address
- Phone number and email
- Logo for visual customization

Prisma also supports **automatic address lookup via ZIP code (CEP)** to speed up form filling and reduce errors.

---

### Client Information

The same structured flow applies to client data:

- Name or company name
- CPF or CNPJ
- Full address
- Phone and email
- Automatic ZIP code lookup

This makes the quoting process **faster and more standardized**, especially for professionals who generate quotes frequently.

---

### Items, Payment and Notes

Additional features include:

- Unlimited item entries
- Custom description, quantity, and price
- Automatic total calculations
- Multiple payment options (Pix, card, bank transfer, boleto, or cash)
- Notes field for deadlines, conditions, or additional information
